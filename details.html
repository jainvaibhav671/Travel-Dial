Tour Overview

Uttarakhand or also known as devbhoomi, is one of the holiest and the most scenic hill states in india. Crowned with the snow-clad Himalayas, Uttarakhand has always been a hot spot for tourists, pilgrims and travellers from all over the world. Due to its incredible spiritual and mythological accounts dating back to the ancient ages, Uttarakhand is a jewel of a holiday destination. One can learn a lot about not just spirituality but also the culture, way of life and traditions that make India a unique nation.

  • Ram / Laxman jhula visit and exploration
  • Full day rishikesh sightseeing with temple visit
